The Process

Rushing

This week is meant for meeting the active members on our campus and learning about our organization's mission as a whole.

Interviews

Following rush week, we're gonna be meeting with some of you to learn more about you and your ambitions. Chances of obtaining an interview can reflect on how many rush events were attended.

Bidding

Post-Interviews, rushees will be obtaining bids from Active Members. What a bid is by definition is a formal invitation in order to join our organization. Very few have the opportunity to achieve this.

Classified

Information moving forward is available only to those who recieve a bid.

Eligibiity Requirements:

Undergraduate in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering or Computer Science

18 years of age

GPA is 2.0 or above
